How to Play

  • Select a difficulty level.
  • Click "Start Playing" to begin the game.
  • A word will be displayed.
  • Type the word as quickly as possible in the input field.
  • Type the same number of letters as the displayed word to proceed to the next word, regardless of correctness.
  • Try to type as many words as you can within the given time.
  • Your score will be displayed under and at the end of the game.
